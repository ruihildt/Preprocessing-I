I'm a stable-boy at Winterfell. I am known as Hodor because that is the only word I'm capable of saying. My height is over seven feet tall, and I may have giant ancestry. I have a friendly, childlike disposition and possesses great strength, although I'm reluctant to use it against others.

More about me

"Hodor" is a seemingly nonsense word, though in the process it became the name everyone calls me. It is derived from the phrase "hold the door", the words that I heard during the seizure that mentally disabled me as a result of the future Bran Stark accidentally warging into my young self during a vision. Nonetheless, House Stark set me to work at a productive occupation within my capacities, as a stableboy serving at Winterfell. Hodor is incredibly large and strong. While I am slow of wits, I am gentle and loyal to the Starks. I am actually Old Nan's great-grandson and only known relative.
